{% trans 'Quality Control' %}

{% blocktrans %} Amara’s Flexible Workflows offer several ways to ensure outstanding quality for your subtitles. Whether you use internal staff, outside contractors, professional services, or a volunteer community—you’ll be able to create, review, and approve workflows that meet your needs. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans 'Examples of QC processes:' %}

  • {% blocktrans %} Have your contractors work inside your Amara account and then require staff review before subtitles go live. {% endblocktrans %}
  • {% blocktrans %} Some universities use paid captioning services to quickly caption lecture videos and then invite experts in the university community to fix any mistakes with proper names or technical terms. {% endblocktrans %}
  • {% blocktrans %} Volunteer teams can self-manage and reach very high quality by requiring peer review of subtitles before they are published. More senior members of the community can be promoted to a moderator role. {% endblocktrans %}
